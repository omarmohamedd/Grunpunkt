GRÜNACID

Grünacid is increasingly used in poultry industry to control infections with enteropathogens in the flock, to increase palatability of the ration, and to improve the digestion and absorption of the feedstuffs. Additional benefits of Grünacid include the control of respiratory complex , coccidiosis and wet litter in the farm, increase eggshell thickness and egg hatchability. The used organic calcium has a double bioavailability compared to Limestone. The organic acids are used as an additional source of energy.
