MYCOLECT

Mycotoxins are common in most raw materials and finished feeds. They are produced by many types of molds and fungi. They are both polar and non-polar in nature. Therefore, the inactivation of mycotoxins is a diffecult process which requires simultaneouse use of different materials to ensure their complete elimination. Mycotoxins have negative effects on production, reproduction and immunity. They cause damage to the vital organs like liver and kidneys. So, MYCOLECT is powerful and balanced synergistic mixture of carefully selected additives to bind polar mycotoxins and inactivates non-polar mycotoxins in contaminated raw materials and feeds.
